The Challenge

The Infrastructure Bottleneck
in Biotech Education

Biotech students across India and emerging markets master complex theory, yet graduate lacking real, job-ready practical skills.

💸

Prohibitive Cost

Physical wet labs cost crores to set up and maintain — unaffordable for most Tier-2 and Tier-3 institutions and individual students.

🚫

Limited Access

Overcrowded labs, rigid time slots, and geographic barriers restrict who can practice — and how often they can do so.

📉

Skills Gap

Graduates enter the workforce with theoretical knowledge but little hands-on competency, making them unready for industry demands.

⚠️

Safety Risks

Real lab environments carry hazards — chemicals, biohazards, equipment risks — especially for students still building foundational skills.

Our Story

We Didn't Accept the
Infrastructure Bottleneck

HelixSkillz was born from a simple but painful observation: biotech students across India master complex theory, yet graduate lacking real, job-ready practical skills. Physical wet labs are expensive, overcrowded, time-restricted, and inaccessible — especially in Tier-2 and Tier-3 institutions. We decided to solve it.

Our Vision

To become the global standard virtual lab ecosystem for biotech education and workforce readiness.

Our Mission

To make high-quality, practical biotech education accessible, affordable, and industry-relevant for students worldwide — removing dependence on physical lab infrastructure.

Our Philosophy

Scientific excellence should not be limited by geography, budget, or infrastructure. Every ambitious student deserves the chance to develop real lab skills.
